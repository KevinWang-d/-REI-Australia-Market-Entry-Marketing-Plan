# REI Australia Market Entry Marketing Plan

A team-based international marketing project focused on developing a market entry strategy for **REI Co-op** in Australia.

## Overview

This project examined the feasibility of REI entering the Australian outdoor retail market and developed a tailored international marketing plan based on market demand, consumer behavior, competition, sustainability trends, and financial projections.

Our team evaluated country selection criteria, assessed Australia’s outdoor industry landscape, analyzed target segments and market positioning, and proposed product, pricing, distribution, and promotion strategies to support REI’s expansion.

## Project Scope

The project covered:

- International opportunity assessment
- Country screening and market selection
- Australian outdoor market analysis
- Consumer and purchasing behavior
- Competitive landscape and market share review
- SWOT analysis
- Segmentation, targeting, and positioning
- Product, price, place, and promotion strategy
- Budget, break-even analysis, and 5-year action planning

## Key Focus Areas

- Why Australia was selected over other international market options
- How REI’s sustainability-focused brand could fit local outdoor culture
- How consumer preferences, offline shopping behavior, and competition shape entry strategy
- How a differentiated product and communication plan could support market entry
- How financial planning supports feasibility and long-term growth
